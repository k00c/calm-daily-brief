The disruption-plagued Perth-to-Bunbury train service is back in operation following years of breakdowns and maintenance delays. The service resumption marks a significant milestone for regional transport in Western Australia, reconnecting the two key regional centres after extended downtime.

Despite the reopening, local feedback suggests some frustration remains about the timing and scope of the rollout. Many residents had been waiting years for reliable service restoration.

The restored connection is expected to improve transport links and accessibility for commuters and businesses between Perth and Bunbury.
