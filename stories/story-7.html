A new approach to cancer screening uses artificial intelligence trained on bowel screening samples to improve detection accuracy. Researchers plan to use samples that are normally discarded after routine testing, repurposing this material to train machine learning systems. The technology offers potential to identify early warning signs of bowel cancer more reliably than existing methods. This application of AI demonstrates how medical research can use existing resources to develop tools that may improve health outcomes and catch disease at earlier, more treatable stages.
