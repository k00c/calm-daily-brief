Health New Zealand Te Whatu Ora has announced plans to address concerns after Palmerston North's last gastroenterologist is departing. The loss of the specialist creates a gap in local digestive health services for the regional community.

Health New Zealand says it wants to reassure the public that gastroenterology services will continue and that recruitment efforts are underway to fill the position. The organization is communicating with the community about transition arrangements and how patients will access ongoing care.

The situation highlights broader challenges facing regional health services in recruiting and retaining specialist doctors, particularly in smaller provincial centres.
