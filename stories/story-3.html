Hundreds of residents have been cut off following storm damage that washed out a critical bridge connecting communities. The isolated populations are expected to remain without direct road access for several days, with some areas also experiencing power outages.

Authorities are exploring alternative supply routes to deliver food and essential supplies to affected communities. Options being considered include boat access and air transport where ground routes are impassable.

Response teams are working to assess the full extent of damage and prioritize access restoration to the most vulnerable populations.
